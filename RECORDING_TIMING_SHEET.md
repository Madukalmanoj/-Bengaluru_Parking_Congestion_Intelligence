## 🎤 Voice-Over Sync Tips

### Method 1: Record Audio While Recording Screen
- Narrate while you record
- Keep voice-over script visible
- May need to re-record if mistakes

### Method 2: Add Voice-Over Later (RECOMMENDED)
1. Record all screen footage first (silent)
2. Generate voice-overs in Firefly for each scene
3. Sync audio with video in editor
4. Easier to get perfect audio quality

## ✅ Pre-Recording Test

### Test Recording (30 seconds):
1. Record Scene 1 as a test
2. Check video quality
3. Check cursor visibility
4. Check if UI is clear and readable
5. Verify recording area captures full browser window
6. If good → proceed with full recording
7. If issues → adjust settings and retry

---

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Moving mouse too fast** → Move smoothly
❌ **Clicking too quickly** → Pause after each click
❌ **Zooming too fast** → Smooth, slow zoom
❌ **Not waiting for load** → Wait 1-2s after clicks
❌ **Cursor off important elements** → Hover on what you discuss
❌ **Recording notifications** → Disable all popups
❌ **Background apps visible** → Close everything else
❌ **Voice too fast** → Speak at moderate pace
❌ **Music too loud** → Keep background music at 15-20% volume

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Rehearse** each scene 2-3 times before recording
2. **Record extra footage** - better to have more than less
3. **Keep cursor movements smooth** and purposeful
4. **Pause between scenes** when recording continuously
5. **Use keyboard shortcuts** (if smoother than mouse)
6. **Record in one session** for consistent lighting/colors
7. **Save project file** in video editor before exporting
8. **Test export** with a short 10-second clip first
